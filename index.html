I am a Lecturer in School of Information Technology at Deakin Universityand an Honorary Fellow in School of Computing and Information Systems at The University of Melbourne. My main research area is trustworthy machine learning, which studies the generalization, robustness, trustworthiness and interpretability aspects of the mainstream machine learning algorithms and models. My research is also closely realated to data privacy, AI security and AI ethics. My passion is to make the world a better place for every life using AI. I received my Ph.D. degree from The University of Melbourne, advised by Prof. James Bailey and Dr. Sudanthi Wijewickrema. I also worked for 1.8 years at The University of Melbourne as a postdoctoral research fellow. I obainted my Bacholor and Master degrees from Jilin University and Tsinghua University, respectively.
